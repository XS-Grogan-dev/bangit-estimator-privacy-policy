BangIt Estimator

Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how BangIt Services and our BangIt Estimator application ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Customer Information: When creating estimates, you may optionally provide customer details including name, phone number, email address, and address for PDF generation.
  • Estimate Data: The app stores your project estimates, including selected items, quantities, and calculated totals locally on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information automatically
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services

How We Use Information

The information you provide is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing your cost estimates
  • Generating PDF reports with customer information (when provided)
  • Storing your estimates locally on your device

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All estimate data is stored locally on your device using secure device storage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on external servers or in the cloud
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete estimates at any time
  • Device Security: Data security depends on your device's built-in security features

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • When you choose to export and share PDF estimates using your device's sharing capabilities
  • Data shared through PDF export is under your direct control

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or update your estimates at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual estimates or clear all data
  • Export: You can export your data as PDF files

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • Company: BangIt Services
  • Email: support@bangitservices.com
  • App Support: Available through the App Store

For technical support, please use the support options available through your device's App Store.
